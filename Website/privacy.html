Privacy Policy

BudgetThing is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All data you enter (such as transactions and categories) is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple’s on-device storage. The data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up with your own device backups.

Analytics & Tracking

BudgetThing does not include analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, or third-party code that collects data.

Accounts

No account or login is required. We do not operate servers for this app.
